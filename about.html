ABOUT ME

Who am I?

My name is Frederic Pun, your typical 19 year old pursuing his interest in the field of computer science. This interest, if I do say, comes from possibility my father who is also affiliated with the field, as well as a journey fueled by curiousity. As a kid, I have always been fascinated with technology and it's endless, growing capabilities that seems to now be incorporated into everything in every way possible. Being born in Canada, yet not living there at the time, I worked my way from my highschool to continue my journey here in Canada in one of the best university around, The University of Toronto Scaborough.



"Life is a journey, not a destination."
- Ralph Waldo Emerson

Academic Life

During my time at my highschool, there was no computer science courses, much less anything focused on coding. The closest thing offered was electronics and robotics using Arduino, and even with that it was limited. Hence, with a goal in mind to pursue computer science, I went out and took a Java course which exposed me to the world of Computer Science - So I thought. After applying for university, I get a message from my school that something has arrived for me - An envolope for The University of Toronto for Co-op Computer Science. From there on, from all the oportunities and studies, I realized that I've only scratched the surface and found myself anew as I indulge myself into the world of Computer Science.
